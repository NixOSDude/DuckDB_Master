// HOW IT WORKS

Your data stays in your cloud.
My compute does the work.

You grant read access to your S3 bucket, Azure Blob container, or GCP bucket. I spin up a high-performance compute node — locally or on a cheap cloud VM — and DuckDB reads your data directly from object storage. No copying. No cluster. Results delivered as Parquet, CSV, or a live Streamlit dashboard.

DuckDB reads Parquet directly from object storage — no full download required for most analytical queries. Columnar predicate pushdown means only the columns and rows your query touches are transferred.
